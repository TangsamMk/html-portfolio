The Best Movies According to Tangsam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Least of These

Drama film based on the true story of Graham Staines, an Australian missionary working in India helping lepers. Film based on the passage that Jesus said, "As you did this to the least of these my brothers, you did it to me." - Mathew 25:40

War Room: Prayer is a Powerful Weapon

Christian Drama that focuses on the power of prayer in transforming broken lives and marriages. It encourages me to pray for my family

David

An animated movie from the Bible. Young David's faith in GOD gives him victory over Goliath.
